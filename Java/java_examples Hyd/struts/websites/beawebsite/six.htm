BEA WebLogic Platform 8.1 Delivers Faster Time-to-Value

By seamlessly converging the process of application development and application integration into a single, simplified approach, BEA allows customers to realize significantly faster time-to-value on their mission-critical business integration projects.

  • Reduce Development Time: BEA WebLogic Platform 8.1 saves time and money by eliminating the integration of disparate application and integration technologies, allowing IT organizations to focus on addressing business goals, instead of the underlying infrastructure.
  • Reduce J2EE Complexity: BEA WebLogic Platform 8.1 makes J2EE more accessible to a broader range of developers, allowing IT organizations to use developer resources more flexibly and effectively.
  • Increase Developer Productivity: BEA WebLogic Platform 8.1 includes a unified development environment, which provides a common environment for collaborative development, ensures interoperability and reuse of skills and software, and minimizes training costs, risks, and errors.
  • Enable Change at the Speed of Business: BEA WebLogic Platform 8.1 delivers a comprehensive platform for enterprise Web Services, service-oriented architectures, and control-based development environments, providing IT organizations with a scalable, adaptable platform that simplifies integration of enterprise resources into applications and business processes.
